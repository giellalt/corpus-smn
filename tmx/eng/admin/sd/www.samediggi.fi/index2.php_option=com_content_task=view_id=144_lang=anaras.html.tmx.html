Aimo Guttorm väljejui Sämitige lahâčällee viirgán Aimo Guttorm appointed Legal Secretary
Guttorm vuoitij jienâstmist vuoigâdvuođâtiettuu kandidaat Siiri Jomppas jienâiguin 11-9. Aimo Guttorm was appointed Legal Secretary for Saami Parliament in Finland 2.6.2008.
